Unit Converter

17 June, 2017 – anematode & Nichodon

This unit converter is exact in every calculation to 16 places and has a vast collection of different units (Source: Wikipedia).
The converter will attempt to guess the unit based on the search query.

NOTE: This program is in the midst of being updated. Some features may not work as expected!
Acceleration, apua, ard, area, ca, charge, c02puv, density, ee, epum, erd, ld, mass, mf, mfs, mr are currently up to date and will convert correctly. However, some of these might be subject to change (additions / removals of units).
