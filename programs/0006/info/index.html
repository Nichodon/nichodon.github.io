Controls

To select a body, click on it. Scroll to zoom in or out on the body, and click and drag to shift the view.
To make selecting a body and viewing a body's position easier, the bodies' sizes have been exaggerated which can be controlled with the "Planetary Size" slider.

Bodies

Currently, the bodies we use are the objects in the Solar System above 450 kilometres and that have been imaged with high detail.
The bodies exaggerated are the ones orbiting around the sun (above 450 km).

Note: we have tried not to engage in any copyright infrigement. However, if you own one of the textures used, please contact us and we will remove it as soon as possible.
We omit textures that have missing portions generally more than 50% (sorry Charon).
Also, we try to use accurate maps that are as close to true color as possible.
The general consensus on the Internet is that Eris is a purple Ganymede, which is why we don't have it yet.
